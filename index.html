Loops
An innovative way to trade your textbooks

How It Works

Before Loops, you had to spend time and effort finding people who had the book you wanted and hope that they wanted the book you were done using. Loops opens you to world of more possibilities with just a few clicks. By comparing the books you list for trade and the books you need with all of our users, Loops automatically generates the smartest trade circles that maximize the number of users who get what they want

About

Loops was created by three aspiring, ambitious, smart, awesome UCLA students to solve a problem that occurred in their own lives - expensive textbooks.

We used Twitter Bootstrap (the Freenlancer Template specifically) along with JavaScript and Node.js to create this website.
